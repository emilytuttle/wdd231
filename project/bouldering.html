Bouldering

Bouldering is a kind of climbing that doesn't require a rope, harness or belayer, but usually requires crash pads, or thick mats to fall on. Some people climb over water instead of crash pads, but either way the idea is to have something beneath you that won't hurt you when you fall on it. Bouldering routes are much shorter than most lead or top rope routes. A lot of bouldering routes are more dynamic and take a lot of power, as opposed to the combined strength and increased endurance necessary for longer climbs.
