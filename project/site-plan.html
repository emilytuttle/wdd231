Project Site Plan

Site Name:

Utah Climbing Information

This is going to be a site about different kinds of climbing and what areas people can do those in.

Site Purpose:

This site is for people who are just starting climbing or don't know about it. It will have information for beginners about what climbs are around them, but will have detailed information about different kinds of climbing for people who don't know about it.

Scenarios:

What is the difference between sport climbing and trad climbing?

What are some of the climbs I can do in the area?

Color Schema:

Green (rgb(0, 148, 94)) will be used for headings with a white font on top. Yellow (rgb(251, 251, 191)) will be used for highlights. Text will be white or black

Typography:

The header will use Arial, the other sections will use Roboto.
